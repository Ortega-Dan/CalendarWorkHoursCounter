Calendar Hours Counter

  • Count Current Day Hours: Ctrl + I
  • Count Week Hours with filter: Ctrl + Shift + K
    (From there you can check for different days according to instructions or query a specific term. All searches are case insensitive).

Works on Google Calendar for personal and enterprise versions, with English (US) language setting, and counting events of less than 24 hours long.

It works in Week view of the calendar
(Switch to week view by hitting "w" key [Which is google-calendar's native functionality]).

It compares against 8 hours for current-day, or specific day queries, or counts hours for the entire week for empty queries.

To ignore events write cal.ignore somewhere in the event title.
