Calendar Hours Counter

  • Count Current Day Hours: Alt + I
  • Count Week Hours with filter: Alt + K
    (From there you can check for different days according to instructions).

Works on Google Calendar for personal and enterprise versions, with English (US) language, and United States country settings, and counts events that start and end on the same day (e.g events that don't start in a day and end in the next day or after, according to the time zone presented in the Google Calendar view evaluated).

It works in Week view of the calendar
(Switch to week view by hitting "w" key [Which is google-calendar's native functionality]).

It compares against 8 hours for current-day, and counts hours for the entire week for empty queries.

To ignore events write cal.ignore somewhere in the event title. It also automatically ignores declined and tentative attendance ("maybe") events.

To generate hours reports, out of the exported iCalendar (ics file) from Google Calendar settings, it is recommended to prefix events with the format "CLIENT: Project, " where both "CLIENT" and "Project" are made of ASCII letters, numbers, or underscore.
The report can be generated by using BasicICSreader from: https://github.com/Ortega-Dan/BasicIcsCalendarReader
